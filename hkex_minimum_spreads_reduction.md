## 4. Changes from Original to Phase 1

| Security Price Range (HK$) | Original Tick Size (HK$) | Phase 1 Tick Size (HK$) | Change Magnitude |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **> 10.00 to 20.00** | 0.020 | **0.010** | **-50%** |
| **> 20.00 to 50.00** | 0.050 | **0.020** | **-60%** |

*(The price band >20.00 to 100.00 was split. The segment up to 50.00 was reduced to 0.020, while the segment >50.00 to 100.00 remains at the original 0.050).*
